Omakase (お任せ)

A Japanese phrase that means "I'll leave it to you" and is derived from makaseru (任せる) which means "to entrust".

Juno's Omakasea is a private seven-seat chef's table where you let go and Chef B.K. Park creates your dining experience.

Reservations are by phone only and require 48 hours' notice. The price per person is based on current market prices and does not include alcohol
